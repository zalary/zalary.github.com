Vim Goodness

While getting back into code, I am also getting into vim. When I used it previously, I pretty much knew ‘i’, which just leads to a lot of frustration. This time, since I’m coding mostly via ssh to my ubunu vm, I’m much more reliant on the terminal for navigating and coding, so I’m taking the time to learn the myriad of keyboard commands that make vim rock.

And now that I’ve installed pathogen and figured out where to put/adjust things, I can piddle even more!

I’m just scratching the surface at this point, but even so, I can see how it’s a total game-changer.
